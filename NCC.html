National Cadet Corps

Services

The National Cadet Corps (NCC) at KTS School is a vibrant and enriching program that instills in cadets a sense of discipline, leadership, and social responsibility. Through rigorous training, cadets learn valuable skills such as drill, parade, map reading, and first aid. They also participate in community service activities, adventure camps, and national level events. NCC cadets develop a strong sense of patriotism, teamwork, and self-discipline. The program provides them with opportunities to explore their potential, build confidence, and become responsible citizens. NCC in KTS School is a valuable experience that equips cadets with the skills and values necessary to succeed in life

Plantation Program in KTS

KTS School has implemented a comprehensive plantation program to promote environmental sustainability and create a greener campus. Students of all ages participate in planting trees and maintaining the school's green spaces. The program fosters a sense of responsibility towards nature and encourages students to contribute to a healthier environment. Through this initiative, KTS School aims to cultivate a culture of environmental awareness and stewardship among its students.
